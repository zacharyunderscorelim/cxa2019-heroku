What isFoodbank?
Thank you for visitingwww.foodbank.sg.
Probably the first question on your mind right now is "What is a Food Bank?".
To make it really simple, a Food Bank is a place where companies or people can come to deposit / donate their unused or unwanted foods which will then be collected and allocated to the Needy via channels such as VWOs, charities and soup kitchens
